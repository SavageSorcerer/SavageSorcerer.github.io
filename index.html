SJBHSMUN is the annual model united-nations conducted by St. Joseph’s Boy’s High School. It provides students with a platform to exercise their skills in topics such as deliberation, delegation and comprehension. The delegates will be confronted with a diverse range of geo-political and socio-political issues to test their prowess at diplomacy and other related criteria.
SJBHS aims to foster an environment of peace, harmony and mutual growth amongst the delegates as they aim to tackle the world’s most pressing issues.

Anti-Harrassment Policy

We here at SJBHS, have a firm stance against harassment of any kind. Keeping in mind the mode of conducting the conference, and the complications which may arise from the same, we have written down some basic guidelines which all delegates must adhere to, failing which they shall be removed from the committee, and not be considered for any awards. The delegation may also be affected if more than one member engages in prohibited activity as mentioned hereunder.

Depending on the severity of the offence the matter may be reported to the PRINCIPAL / TEACHER CO-ORDINATOR of the respective school/college from which the participant hails. NO REFUND WILL BE ISSUED TO THE DELEGATE IN THE AFOREMENTIONED SITUATION.
